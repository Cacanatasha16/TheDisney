Welcome to Disneyland!

Disneyland is a famous theme park that originally from Anaheim, California that was opened in 1955 and the first theme park opened by The Walt Disney Company and the only one designed and constructed under the direct supervision of Walt Disney. Out of the 11 Disney operated parks around the globe, only five are Disneyland while the other six parks are the Magic Kingdom, Animal Kingdom, Tokyo Disney Sea, Disney California Adventure, EPCOT and Disney Hollywood Studios.There are 5 disneyland and it is located at California, Florida, Paris, Hong Kong, Shanghai and Tokyo.

DISNEYLAND

When you enter the park, get ready to be serenaded by Disney characters. Riding on historic vehicles and horse-drawn carriages, Main Street USA lets visitors experience 19th-century America. At Fantasyland, you can find Alice and the Mad Hatter, and Sleeping Beauty has a castle. Take a ride on Big Thunder Mountain, one of Disney World's most well-known attractions.

DISNEYSEA

Tokyo is home not only to Disneyland but also to Disney Sea. The park has 7 different harbour that resemble different topographical areas leaving you with a lot of options to explore. Look for Ariel at the Mermaid Lagoon, or journey to the center of the earth at the Mysterious Island. Tokyo Disneyland with Disney Sea make it the third most visited park in the world. Watch out for the Legend of Mythica show to take you on a magical journey, and the Fantasmic show for Mickey and his antics.
